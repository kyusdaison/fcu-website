Scholarships & funding奖学金与资助

Support for your studies.为你的学习提供支持。

The University recognises talent, motivation and potential. A range of scholarship and funding options may be available depending on the programme, your background and your circumstances. To discuss what you may be eligible for, contact the admissions office.大学重视才华、动力与潜力。视课程、背景与具体情况，可能提供多种奖学金与资助方案。如需了解你可能符合的条件，请联系招生办公室。

Payment付款

Paying your fees.缴纳学费。

Payment arrangements, including any instalment options, are confirmed with your offer. The admissions office can explain accepted methods and the timeline for your intake.付款安排（包括任何分期方式）将随录取通知确认。招生办公室可说明你所在入学季的受理方式与时间安排。
