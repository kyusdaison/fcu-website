Design & Media设计与媒体 (DProf)

A doctorate for senior creative leaders and practitioners — producing original, doctoral-level research on real problems of design, media and communication.为资深创意领导者与实践者而设的博士学位——就设计、媒体与传播的真实问题，产出博士层级的原创研究。

Why this programme课程定位

Research from creative practice.从创意实践出发的研究。

The hardest questions in design, media and communication are met by the people who lead the work — who rarely have the framework to research them at depth.设计、媒体与传播中最棘手的问题，往往落在引领工作的人身上——他们却少有框架去深入研究。

This doctorate gives senior practitioners the methods and supervision to turn a problem of practice into original, rigorous and defensible research, including through practice.本博士为资深从业者提供方法与导师指导，把实践问题转化为原创、严谨而经得起检验的研究——并可通过实践进行。

Who it's for适合人群

For senior creative leaders and practitioners.为资深创意领导者与从业者而设。

Senior designers, communicators, media and creative leaders who want to research a problem of practice at doctoral level.希望在博士层级研究实践问题的资深设计师、传播者，以及媒体与创意领导者。

  • Situate a problem of practice within the research literature将实践问题置于研究文献中定位
Research研究能力

Leadership领导力
  • Translate research into creative and organisational change将研究转化为创意与组织变革
  • Lead responsibly and with evidence以证据为本、负责任地领导
Programme structure项目结构

Doctoral study built around your practice.围绕你的实践而构建的博士研究。

A structured taught and research-training phase, followed by an original practice research thesis.先是结构化的授课与研究训练阶段，继而完成一篇原创的实践研究论文。

Taught phase授课阶段
Structured · methods & advanced study结构化 · 方法与高阶学习
Advanced Design & Media Theory · Research Design for Practice · Doctoral Methods & Ethics高级设计与媒体理论 · 面向实践的研究设计 · 博士方法与伦理
Research phase研究阶段
Supervised practice research导师指导的实践研究
A sustained, supervised research project (which may be practice-based) on a problem of design, media or communication.一项持续的、导师指导的研究项目（可为实践型），针对设计、媒体或传播的问题。
Thesis & viva论文与答辩
Doctoral thesis博士论文
An original thesis (with practice, where relevant) defended by viva voce.一篇原创论文（必要时含实践成果），通过口试（viva）答辩。

Designed to be undertaken alongside a senior creative or practice role.可与资深创意或实务岗位并行修读。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Cohort-based residencies and seminars, individual supervision, and a community of senior practitioners. Assessment is by research milestones, the thesis (with practice, where relevant) and viva voce.以同期同学为核心的集中授课与研讨、个人导师指导，以及资深从业者社群。评估以研究里程碑、论文（必要时含实践成果）与口试答辩为主。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A Master's degree (or equivalent) and substantial senior professional experience; a research area aligned to practice. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.硕士学位(或同等)且具备丰富的资深专业经验；研究方向需与实践相关。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Advancement into the most senior creative, design, media and communication leadership roles, with research that strengthens practice.迈向最高层级的创意、设计、媒体与传播领导岗位，并以研究强化实践。

A terminal doctoral qualification for senior practice.面向资深实践的终极博士资历。
