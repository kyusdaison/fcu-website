Why this programme课程定位

Work at the frontier.在前沿之处探索。

The questions shaping the future of design, media and communication have no settled answers — they need original research, including through practice.塑造设计、媒体与传播未来的那些问题，尚无定论——它们需要原创研究，并可通过实践进行。

FCU's PhD supports rigorous doctoral work across design, media and creative systems, supervised by faculty active in these fields.FCU 的博士项目支持跨越设计、媒体与创意系统的严谨博士研究，由活跃于这些领域的师资指导。

Who it's for适合人群

For the next generation of researchers.为下一代研究者而设。

Outstanding graduates with a relevant Master's and a clear, original research proposal, pursuing an academic or advanced research career.持相关硕士学位、具备清晰原创研究计划，有志于学术或高阶研究生涯的优秀毕业生。

  • Design and conduct independent, ethical, original research独立设计并开展合乎伦理的原创研究

Professional专业能力

Programme structure项目结构

Supervised original research.导师指导下的原创研究。

Research training, sustained independent research, and a thesis defended by viva.研究训练、持续的独立研究，以及通过口试答辩的学位论文。

Year 1第一年
Research training & proposal研究训练与计划
Research methods and training, and confirmation of the research proposal.研究方法与训练，以及研究计划的确认。
Years 2–3第二至三年
Original research原创研究

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Individual and team supervision, a structured research-training programme, and an active seminar and studio research culture. Assessment is by progression milestones, the thesis and viva voce.个人与团队导师指导、结构化的研究训练计划，以及活跃的研讨与工作室研究文化。评估以阶段里程碑、论文与口试答辩为主。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A relevant Master's degree at merit/distinction (or equivalent) and a strong, original research proposal. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5 (or higher for some areas).相关硕士学位且成绩良好/优异(或同等)，并有扎实、原创的研究计划。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5(部分方向要求更高)。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Academic and research careers, and senior creative, strategy and leadership roles across the creative industries and beyond.学术与研究生涯，以及创意产业及其他领域的高阶创意、战略与领导岗位。

The faculty's terminal research degree.本院的终极研究型学位。
