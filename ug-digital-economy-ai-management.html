Honours Degree · Levels 4–6荣誉学位 · 第 4–6 级

Digital Economy, Artificial Intelligence & Management数字经济、人工智能与管理 (BSc Hons)

An interfaculty honours degree for those who will build and lead in the digital economy — combining economics, artificial intelligence and management across the University's five faculties.一门跨学院的荣誉学位，为那些将在数字经济中建设与引领的人而设——将经济学、人工智能与管理跨越五大学院融为一体。

Why this degree学位定位

Lead in the digital economy.引领数字经济。

The digital economy is being built at the intersection of economics, artificial intelligence and management — yet most degrees teach these apart.数字经济正诞生于经济学、人工智能与管理的交汇处——而多数学位却将它们分而教之。

This honours degree is interfaculty by design: you learn to understand markets, build with technology and lead organisations, as one connected education.本荣誉学位以跨学院为设计原则:你将理解市场、用技术构建、并领导组织，作为一段彼此相连的完整教育。

Who it's for适合人群

For the builders of the digital economy.为数字经济的建设者而设。

Ambitious school-leavers and transfer students who want a rigorous, interdisciplinary degree spanning economics, technology and management — and an international outlook.志向远大的中学毕业生与转学学生，渴望一门严谨、跨学科、横跨经济、技术与管理，并具国际视野的学位。

Programme structure项目结构

360 credits across three years.三年 · 360 学分。

An interfaculty foundation, a specialist core, and an honours year — Levels 4 to 6.跨学院的基础、专业核心，以及荣誉之年——第 4 级至第 6 级。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Lectures, seminars, studios, labs and group projects, with an interfaculty capstone in the final year. Assessment blends coursework, projects and examinations.讲授、研讨、工作室、实验与小组项目，并在最后一年完成一个跨学院的毕业项目。评估融合课程作业、项目与考试。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

School-leaving qualifications such as A-levels, the International Baccalaureate, or an equivalent recognised qualification. English equivalent to IELTS 6.0. International applicants are warmly welcomed.A-Level、IB（国际文凭）或同等受认可的中学毕业资历。英语相当于 IELTS 6.0。诚挚欢迎国际申请者。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Roles across the digital economy — technology, finance, consulting, startups, management and the public sector — and a strong foundation for postgraduate study.数字经济中的各类岗位——科技、金融、咨询、初创、管理与公共部门——并为研究生阶段打下坚实基础。

Progression to the University's Master's programmes (MSc, MA and MRes) across all five faculties.可衔接本校五大学院的硕士课程（MSc、MA 与 MRes）。
