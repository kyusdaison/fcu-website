Programmes · Every route课程 · 全部路径

Programmes across the whole university.贯穿全校的课程体系。

From undergraduate honours degrees to taught and research Master's and the doctorate — one academic ladder, organised across five faculties.从本科荣誉学位，到授课型与研究型硕士，直至博士——一套学位阶梯，跨越五大学院共同组织。

Academic pathways学术路径

Programmes are organised as a ladder of inquiry and capability.课程被组织为探究与能力进阶的阶梯。

Each route combines disciplinary depth with interfaculty connection. Students move from foundations and methods into advanced projects, supervised research and doctoral-level contribution, while remaining connected to the real systems their work seeks to understand.每一条路径都把学科深度与跨学院连接结合起来。学生从基础与方法进入高阶项目、研究指导与博士层面的贡献，同时始终连接其研究试图理解的真实系统。

Honours foundation荣誉学位基础

Three-year undergraduate degrees build breadth, method and judgement.三年制本科学位奠定广度、方法与判断力。

Master's depth硕士阶段深化

Taught programmes deepen expertise through studios, seminars and applied projects.授课型硕士通过工作室、研讨与应用项目深化专业能力。

Research formation研究能力形成

MRes routes prepare students for advanced inquiry and independent research design.MRes 路径培养高阶探究与独立研究设计能力。

Doctoral contribution博士层面贡献

Doctoral study asks for original work with intellectual and practical consequence.博士学习要求形成具有思想与实践影响的原创工作。

The programmes page is organised to make academic progression clear: undergraduate honours study, taught Master's routes, MRes research formation, professional doctorates and PhD study.课程页按照清晰的学术进阶组织：本科荣誉学位、授课型硕士、MRes 研究能力形成、专业博士与 PhD 研究。

01

Level层级

Levels 4–6, 7 and 8 show the move from foundation to advanced contribution.第 4–6 级、第 7 级与第 8 级呈现从基础到高阶贡献的进阶。

02

Mode学习方式

Taught, research and doctoral routes serve different academic purposes.授课型、研究型与博士路径服务于不同的学术目的。

03

Faculty学院

Each programme is anchored in a faculty while remaining connected to the University as a whole.每门课程锚定于一个学院，同时与整所大学保持连接。

04

Research depth研究深度

MRes, professional doctorate and PhD routes mark increasing independence in inquiry.MRes、专业博士与 PhD 标示探究独立性的逐步提升。

Study modes学习方式

Different modes support different kinds of academic work.不同学习方式支持不同类型的学术工作。

The University's taught, research and doctoral routes are arranged so that students can choose the form of study that best matches their current question, professional direction and level of independence.大学的授课型、研究型与博士路径经过清晰组织，使学生能够根据自己的问题意识、专业方向与独立程度选择合适的学习形式。

01

Taught study授课型学习

Structured seminars, studios and applied projects help students build advanced knowledge with guided intellectual pace.结构化研讨、工作室与应用项目帮助学生在明确节奏中建立高阶知识。

02

Research study研究型学习

MRes routes place methods, evidence and research design at the centre of the student experience.MRes 路径把方法、证据与研究设计置于学生体验的中心。

03

Professional doctorate专业博士

Professional doctorates connect advanced inquiry to complex practice, leadership and institutional decision-making.专业博士把高阶探究连接到复杂实践、领导力与机构决策。

04

PhD researchPhD 研究

PhD study is designed for original contribution, sustained supervision and independent scholarly judgement.PhD 研究面向原创贡献、持续导师指导与独立学术判断。
