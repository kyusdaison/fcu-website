Programmes · Every route课程 · 全部路径

Programmes across the whole university.贯穿全校的课程体系。

From undergraduate honours degrees to taught and research Master's and the doctorate — one academic ladder, organised across five faculties.从本科荣誉学位，到授课型与研究型硕士，直至博士——一套学位阶梯，跨越五大学院共同组织。

Academic pathways学术路径

Programmes are organised as a ladder of inquiry and capability.课程被组织为探究与能力进阶的阶梯。

Each route combines disciplinary depth with interfaculty connection. Students move from foundations and methods into advanced projects, supervised research and doctoral-level contribution, while remaining connected to the real systems their work seeks to understand.每一条路径都把学科深度与跨学院连接结合起来。学生从基础与方法进入高阶项目、研究指导与博士层面的贡献，同时始终连接其研究试图理解的真实系统。

Honours foundation荣誉学位基础

Three-year undergraduate degrees build breadth, method and judgement.三年制本科学位奠定广度、方法与判断力。

Master's depth硕士阶段深化

Taught programmes deepen expertise through studios, seminars and applied projects.授课型硕士通过工作室、研讨与应用项目深化专业能力。

Research formation研究能力形成

MRes routes prepare students for advanced inquiry and independent research design.MRes 路径培养高阶探究与独立研究设计能力。

Doctoral contribution博士层面贡献

Doctoral study asks for original work with intellectual and practical consequence.博士学习要求形成具有思想与实践影响的原创工作。

Choosing a route选择路径

Start with the question you are ready to pursue.从你已经准备追问的问题出发。

Use the programme map to compare academic stage, faculty home, mode of study and research depth before moving into individual programme pages.先通过课程地图比较学术阶段、学院归属、学习方式与研究深度，再进入具体课程页面。

01

Academic stage学术阶段

Begin with where you are in the ladder: honours, Master's, MRes, professional doctorate or PhD.先确认你所在的学位阶梯位置：荣誉学位、硕士、MRes、专业博士或 PhD。

02

Faculty home学院归属

Choose the faculty that gives your question its strongest disciplinary base.选择能够为你的问题提供最有力学科基础的学院。

03

Mode of study学习方式

Taught, research and doctoral routes support different kinds of pace, supervision and independence.授课型、研究型与博士路径对应不同节奏、指导方式与独立程度。

04

Research depth研究深度

Move toward MRes, professional doctorate or PhD study when the central task is sustained inquiry.当核心任务转向持续性探究时，可进入 MRes、专业博士或 PhD 路径。

Entry points进入路径

Different students enter the academic ladder at different points.不同学生从学位阶梯的不同位置进入。

The programme structure helps prospective students, graduates, professionals and research candidates identify the route that matches their current preparation and academic purpose.课程结构帮助未来学生、毕业生、专业人士与研究申请者找到符合其当前准备程度与学术目标的路径。

01

School-leavers and transfers高中毕业生与转学生

Begin with undergraduate honours study when the priority is breadth, foundation and disciplined specialisation over three years.若重点是三年内建立广度、基础与有纪律的专精，可从本科荣誉学位开始。

02

Graduates seeking depth寻求深化的毕业生

Use taught Master's routes to deepen professional knowledge through seminars, studios and applied projects.通过授课型硕士，在研讨、工作室与应用项目中深化专业知识。

03

Emerging researchers研究能力形成者

Choose MRes study when methods, evidence and research design are central to the next academic step.当方法、证据与研究设计成为下一阶段核心时，可选择 MRes 路径。

04

Senior practitioners资深从业者

Professional doctorates and PhD routes support advanced inquiry, supervision and original contribution.专业博士与 PhD 路径支持高阶探究、导师指导与原创贡献。

Study modes学习方式

Different modes support different kinds of academic work.不同学习方式支持不同类型的学术工作。

The University's taught, research and doctoral routes are arranged so that students can choose the form of study that best matches their current question, professional direction and level of independence.大学的授课型、研究型与博士路径经过清晰组织，使学生能够根据自己的问题意识、专业方向与独立程度选择合适的学习形式。

01

Taught study授课型学习

Structured seminars, studios and applied projects help students build advanced knowledge with guided intellectual pace.结构化研讨、工作室与应用项目帮助学生在明确节奏中建立高阶知识。

02

Research study研究型学习

MRes routes place methods, evidence and research design at the centre of the student experience.MRes 路径把方法、证据与研究设计置于学生体验的中心。

03

Professional doctorate专业博士

Professional doctorates connect advanced inquiry to complex practice, leadership and institutional decision-making.专业博士把高阶探究连接到复杂实践、领导力与机构决策。

04

PhD researchPhD 研究

PhD study is designed for original contribution, sustained supervision and independent scholarly judgement.PhD 研究面向原创贡献、持续导师指导与独立学术判断。

Academic calendar学术日历

A clear academic rhythm supports serious study.清晰的学术节奏支撑严肃学习。

Programme pages describe awards and credits; the academic year gives that work a disciplined rhythm of preparation, teaching, project work and review.课程页面说明学位与学分；学年安排则为这些学习提供准备、教学、项目实践与审阅的有序节奏。

01

Orientation and advising导入与指导

Students begin with academic orientation, programme advising and confirmation of the route, level and expectations that apply to their study.学生从学术导入、课程指导，以及对自身路径、层级与学习期待的确认开始。

02

Seminars and studios研讨与工作室

Teaching periods concentrate structured seminars, studios, laboratories and supervised discussion around the stated learning outcomes.教学阶段围绕既定学习成果集中安排结构化研讨、工作室、实验与导师指导下的讨论。

03

Projects and research项目与研究

Independent and collaborative work gives students time to test methods, develop evidence and connect academic questions to real systems.独立与协作工作让学生有时间检验方法、发展证据，并把学术问题连接到真实系统。

04

Review and progression审阅与进阶

Assessment, feedback and progression review help students understand achievement, next steps and readiness for further study.评估、反馈与进阶审阅帮助学生理解学习成果、下一步安排以及继续深造的准备程度。

Research routes研究路线

Three routes support different forms of advanced inquiry.三条路径支持不同形态的高阶探究。

MRes, professional doctorate and PhD routes all involve supervision and research discipline, but they differ in purpose, audience and expected contribution.MRes、专业博士与 PhD 都包含导师指导与研究训练，但它们在目的、面向对象与预期贡献上各有不同。

01

MRes formationMRes 研究形成

A research Master's builds methods, evidence and proposal discipline for students preparing for doctoral study or research-intensive roles.研究型硕士建立方法、证据与研究计划能力，适合准备博士学习或研究密集型工作的学生。

02

Professional inquiry专业实践探究

Professional doctorates are designed for experienced practitioners who want to turn a serious problem of practice into doctoral-level research.专业博士面向有经验的实践者，帮助其把严肃的实践问题转化为博士层级研究。

03

PhD contributionPhD 原创贡献

PhD study is centred on an original contribution to knowledge, sustained scholarly independence and a thesis defended through formal examination.PhD 以对知识的原创贡献、持续的学术独立性，以及通过正式考核的学位论文为核心。

04

Supervision fit导师匹配

Applicants should consider the faculty, question, method and form of contribution before selecting the route that best fits their purpose.申请者应在选择路径前综合考虑学院、问题、方法与贡献形式是否匹配自身目标。

Reading a programme page如何阅读课程页面

Each programme page follows the same academic logic.每个课程页面遵循同一套学术逻辑。

The common page structure lets students compare awards without losing the specific character of each field. Read level, credit, structure and progression together rather than as isolated facts.统一页面结构让学生能够比较不同学位，同时不丢失每个领域自身的特点。请把层级、学分、结构与进阶路径作为整体来阅读，而不是孤立信息。

01

Level and award层级与学位

Level indicates the academic stage; the award shows the kind of study and contribution expected.层级说明学术阶段；学位名称说明学习类型与预期贡献。

02

Credit and duration学分与时长

Credits describe academic volume and help students understand the scale of coursework, project and research expectations.学分说明学术体量，帮助学生理解课程作业、项目与研究要求的规模。

03

Structure and methods结构与方法

The curriculum section shows how modules, seminars, projects and research activity are sequenced.课程结构说明模块、研讨、项目与研究活动如何按阶段展开。

04

Progression and outcomes进阶与成果

Progression routes connect study with employment, advanced research, doctoral work or public and institutional practice.进阶路径把学习同就业、高阶研究、博士学习或公共与机构实践连接起来。

Admissions readiness申请准备

Prepare the evidence that lets the University understand your academic direction.准备能让大学理解你学术方向的材料。

Applicants should read the relevant programme page first, then prepare evidence of academic preparation, language readiness and the question or professional direction they want to pursue.申请者应先阅读相应课程页面，再准备能够说明学术基础、语言准备程度，以及自己希望追问的问题或专业方向的材料。

01

Academic preparation学术准备

Use transcripts, previous study and relevant experience to show readiness for the level of study selected.通过成绩单、既往学习与相关经历，说明自己已准备好进入所选学术层级。

02

Language readiness语言准备

English requirements are stated on programme pages; applicants should be ready to study, write and discuss at the required level.课程页面列明英语要求；申请者应准备好以相应水平学习、写作与讨论。

03

Portfolio or proposal作品集或研究计划

Some routes benefit from a portfolio, project evidence or a clear research proposal that shows direction and seriousness.部分路径适合提交作品集、项目证据或清晰研究计划，以体现方向与严肃性。

04

Advising questions咨询问题

Before contacting the University, identify the programme, faculty and questions on which academic advice would be most useful.联系大学之前，请先明确课程、学院，以及最需要学术建议的问题。
