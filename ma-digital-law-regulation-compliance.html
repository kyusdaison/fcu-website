Why this programme课程定位

Law for the digital state.为数字国家而立的法律。

Digital law has moved from a niche to the centre of every institution — data, platforms, AI and cross-border flows now define legal and regulatory risk.数字法律已从边缘走向每一个机构的核心——数据、平台、AI 与跨境流动，如今定义着法律与监管风险。

This programme builds practitioners who can both interpret fast-moving regulation and design the compliance frameworks institutions need.本课程培养的人才，既能解读快速演变的监管，也能为机构设计所需的合规框架。

Who it's for适合人群

For those who keep institutions accountable.为守护机构合规的人而设。

Lawyers, compliance and risk professionals, regulators and policy staff — and graduates in law, computing, policy or business moving into digital regulation.律师、合规与风险专业人员、监管者与政策人员，以及法律、计算机、政策或商科背景、转向数字监管的毕业生。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Case method, regulatory clinics, drafting workshops and practitioner-led sessions. Assessment uses legal analyses, drafted instruments, compliance projects and the final capstone rather than closed-book exams.案例教学、监管诊所、文书起草工作坊与实务嘉宾讲授。评估以法律分析、文书拟稿、合规项目与毕业项目为主，而非闭卷考试。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent); a background in law is welcome but not required for applicants with relevant policy, computing or business experience. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.良好荣誉学位(UK 2:1 或同等)；欢迎法律背景，具备相关政策、计算机或商业经验者亦可申请。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Compliance and legal counsel, data-protection officers, regulators, policy and public-affairs roles, and advisory and consultancy positions.合规与法务、数据保护官、监管机构、政策与公共事务岗位，以及顾问与咨询职位。

Progression to the faculty's Professional Doctorate (DGov) or PhD.可衔接本院专业博士（DGov）或哲学博士（PhD）。
