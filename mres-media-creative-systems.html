Media & Creative Systems媒体与创意系统 (MRes)

A research-intensive Master's for those who want to investigate media, design, communication and creative systems.一门研究导向的硕士课程，献给希望探究媒体、设计、传播与创意系统的人。

Award学位
MRes研究型硕士 MRes
Level & credits层级与学分
Level 7 · 180 credits第 7 级 · 180 学分
Duration学制
1 year full-time1 年全日制
Faculty所属学院
Design, Media & Creative Systems设计、媒体与创意系统
Why this programme课程定位

Research media and creative systems.研究媒体与创意系统。

Understanding media, design and creative systems demands rigorous research — including practice-based inquiry.理解媒体、设计与创意系统，需要严谨的研究——包括以实践为本的探究。

This programme trains you to design and carry out original research across media and design, as a foundation for a PhD or a research career.本课程训练你在媒体与设计领域设计并完成原创研究，作为攻读博士或研究生涯的基础。

Who it's for适合人群

For future researchers in media and design.为媒体与设计的未来研究者而设。

Strong graduates preparing for doctoral study or research-intensive roles in academia, the creative industries or practice.基础扎实、准备攻读博士或从事学术、创意产业与实务研究密集型工作的优秀毕业生。

Learning outcomes学习成果

What you'll be able to do.毕业时你将能够……

Research design研究设计
  • Design rigorous, ethical research in media and design在媒体与设计领域设计严谨、合乎伦理的研究
  • Select and apply qualitative, practice-based and other methods选择并运用质性、实践型及其他方法
Technical skills技术能力
  • Gather and analyse evidence, including creative practice收集并分析证据，包括创意实践
  • Produce an original, substantial piece of research完成一项原创而有分量的研究
Professional专业能力
  • Communicate research to scholarly and professional audiences向学术与专业受众传达研究成果
  • Work independently to research-degree standards以研究型学位的标准独立工作
Programme structure项目结构

A research-intensive year.以研究为核心的一年。

Advanced methods, a focused taught element, and a substantial supervised thesis.高级方法、精选授课模块，以及一篇分量十足的导师指导论文。

Research training研究训练
Core · methods核心 · 方法
Research Methods (Qualitative & Practice-based) · Advanced Topics in Media & Design · Research Ethics & Integrity研究方法（质性与实践型） · 媒体与设计高级专题 · 研究伦理与诚信
Field element领域模块
Selected modules精选模块
Modules chosen from the faculty's taught Master's, aligned to your research topic.从本院授课型硕士中选修若干，与你的研究主题对接。
Thesis学位论文
Supervised research · major component导师指导研究 · 主体部分
An original supervised research thesis (which may be practice-based) — the principal element of the programme.一篇原创的、导师指导的研究论文（可为实践型）——本课程的主体。

Designed as a direct pathway to doctoral study (DProf or PhD).本课程亦为博士阶段（DProf 或 PhD）的直接通道。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Research-led supervision, methods and studio workshops, and a seminar-based research community. Assessment is by research outputs — a methods portfolio, a research proposal and the final thesis.以研究为导向的导师指导、方法与工作室工作坊，以及研讨式的研究社群。评估以研究成果为主——方法作品集、研究计划书与最终论文。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in design, media, the humanities or a related discipline; a clear research interest. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.设计、媒体、人文或相关学科的良好荣誉学位(UK 2:1 或同等)；具备明确的研究兴趣。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Doctoral study, and research, strategy and creative-leadership roles in academia and the creative industries.攻读博士，以及学术界与创意产业的研究、战略与创意领导岗位。

A direct route to the faculty's PhD or Professional Doctorate (DProf).直通本院哲学博士（PhD）或专业博士（DProf）。
