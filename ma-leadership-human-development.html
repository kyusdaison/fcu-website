Leadership & Human Development领导力与人类发展 (MA)

Develop people and lead organisations and communities — the theory and practice of human development, leadership and change.培育他人，带领组织与社群——人类发展、领导力与变革的理论与实践。

Why this programme课程定位

Lead, and help people grow.领导他人，并助人成长。

Organisations and communities rise or fall on leadership and on the development of their people.组织与社群的兴衰，系于领导力，也系于对人的培育。

This programme combines the science of human development with the practice of leadership, so you can grow people and lead change with judgement and care.本课程将人类发展的科学与领导力的实践结合起来，让你能够以判断力与关怀去培育他人、引领变革。

Who it's for适合人群

For leaders and developers of people.为领导者与育人者而设。

Current and aspiring leaders, managers, and people-development professionals from any sector or discipline.来自任何行业或学科、现任或有志的领导者、管理者，以及人才发展专业人士。

Learning outcomes学习成果

What you'll be able to do.毕业时你将能够……

Knowledge & understanding知识与理解
  • Understand theories of leadership, learning and human development理解领导力、学习与人类发展的理论
  • Analyse how people, teams and cultures grow and change分析人、团队与文化如何成长与变化
Practical skills实践能力
  • Coach, mentor and develop individuals and teams辅导、指导并培育个人与团队
  • Lead change ethically and effectively合乎伦理且有效地引领变革
Professional专业能力
  • Reflect critically on your own leadership practice对自身的领导实践进行批判性反思
  • Build cultures where people and organisations flourish营造让人与组织共同繁荣的文化
Curriculum课程结构

180 credits across three terms.三学期 · 180 学分。

Foundations, leading change and a substantial project — grounded in real practice.基础、引领变革与一个分量十足的项目——立足于真实实践。

Term 1第一学期
Core modules · 60 credits核心模块 · 60 学分
Foundations of Leadership · Human Development & Learning · Organisational Behaviour · Coaching & Mentoring领导力基础 · 人类发展与学习 · 组织行为 · 辅导与指导
Term 2第二学期
Core · 30 credits核心 · 30 学分
Leading Change · Ethics & Responsible Leadership引领变革 · 伦理与负责任的领导
Options · choose 30 credits选修 · 任选 30 学分
Talent & Capability Development · Culture & Belonging · Cross-Cultural Leadership · Wellbeing & Flourishing · Leadership in the Digital Age人才与能力发展 · 文化与归属 · 跨文化领导 · 福祉与繁荣 · 数字时代的领导力
Term 3第三学期
Capstone · 60 credits毕业项目 · 60 学分
Dissertation, or an applied leadership and development project in an organisation.学位论文，或在某一组织中开展的应用型领导与发展项目。

A shared Future Citizen Core seminar runs through the year, alongside the modules above.贯穿全年的“未来公民核心”研讨，与上述模块并行。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Seminars, reflective practice, coaching workshops and practitioner-led sessions. Assessment is by coursework — reflective and analytical assignments, a leadership project and the final capstone.研讨、反思性实践、辅导工作坊与实务嘉宾讲授。评估以课程作业为主——反思与分析作业、领导力项目，以及毕业项目。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in any discipline; relevant experience is welcomed. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.任何学科的良好荣誉学位(UK 2:1 或同等)；欢迎具备相关经验者。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Leadership and management, human resources, learning and development, coaching, and organisational development across sectors.跨行业的领导与管理、人力资源、学习与发展、辅导，以及组织发展。

Progression to the faculty's Professional Doctorate in Leadership & Human Development (DProf) or PhD.可衔接本院领导力与人类发展专业博士（DProf）或哲学博士（PhD）。
