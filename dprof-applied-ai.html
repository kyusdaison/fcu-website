Professional Doctorate · Level 8专业博士 · 第 8 级

Applied Artificial Intelligence应用人工智能 (DProf)

A doctorate for senior technologists and leaders — producing original, doctoral-level research that applies AI to real problems of practice.为资深技术人员与领导者而设的博士学位——产出将 AI 应用于真实实践问题的、博士层级的原创研究。

≈ 3–4 years, part-time约 3–4 年 · 非全日制

Why this programme课程定位

Research from the front line of practice.从实践前线出发的研究。

The most consequential questions about applying AI are met by the people building and deploying it — who rarely have the framework to research them at depth.关于 AI 应用，最具影响的问题，往往落在构建与部署它的人身上——他们却少有框架去深入研究。

This doctorate gives senior practitioners the methods and supervision to turn applied-AI problems into original, rigorous and defensible research.本博士为资深从业者提供方法与导师指导，把应用 AI 的问题转化为原创、严谨而经得起检验的研究。

Who it's for适合人群

For senior practitioners.为资深从业者而设。

Senior engineers, technical leaders, and data and AI executives and advisors who want to research a problem of applied AI at doctoral level.希望在博士层级研究应用 AI 问题的资深工程师、技术领导者，以及数据与 AI 高管及顾问。

Learning outcomes学习成果

What you'll be able to do.毕业时你将能够……

Knowledge & understanding知识与理解
  • Command the theory and methods underpinning applied AI掌握支撑应用 AI 的理论与方法
  • Situate a problem of practice within the research literature将实践问题置于研究文献中定位
Research研究能力
  • Design and conduct rigorous, ethical applied-AI research设计并开展严谨、合乎伦理的应用 AI 研究
  • Make an original contribution to applied-AI knowledge and practice对应用 AI 的知识与实践作出原创贡献
Leadership领导力
  • Translate research into technical and institutional change将研究转化为技术与机构变革
  • Lead responsibly on AI strategy, safety and ethics在 AI 战略、安全与伦理上负责任地领导
Programme structure项目结构

Doctoral study built around your practice.围绕你的实践而构建的博士研究。

A structured taught and research-training phase, followed by an applied-AI research thesis.先是结构化的授课与研究训练阶段，继而完成一篇应用 AI 研究论文。

Taught phase授课阶段
Structured · methods & advanced study结构化 · 方法与高阶学习
Advanced Applied AI · Research Design for Practice · Doctoral Methods & Ethics高级应用 AI · 面向实践的研究设计 · 博士方法与伦理
Research phase研究阶段
Supervised applied research导师指导的应用研究
A sustained, supervised research project on a problem of applied AI in practice.一项持续的、导师指导的、针对应用 AI 实践问题的研究项目。
Thesis & viva论文与答辩
Doctoral thesis博士论文
An original thesis defended by viva voce.一篇通过口试（viva）答辩的原创论文。

Designed to be undertaken alongside a senior technical or leadership role.可与资深技术或领导岗位并行修读。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Cohort-based residencies and seminars, individual supervision, and a community of senior practitioners. Assessment is by research milestones, the thesis and viva voce.以同期同学为核心的集中授课与研讨、个人导师指导，以及资深从业者社群。评估以研究里程碑、论文与口试答辩为主。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A Master's degree (or equivalent) in computing or a related field and substantial senior professional experience; a research area aligned to practice. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.计算机或相关领域硕士学位(或同等)且具备丰富的资深专业经验；研究方向需与实践相关。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Advancement into the most senior technical, data and AI leadership and advisory roles, with research that strengthens practice.迈向最高层级的技术、数据与 AI 领导及顾问岗位，并以研究强化实践。

A terminal doctoral qualification for senior practice.面向资深实践的终极博士资历。
