Master's by Research · Level 7研究型硕士 · 第 7 级

Governance & Digital Society治理与数字社会 (MRes)

A research-intensive Master's for those who want to investigate, rigorously, how governance and society are being remade by digital systems.一门研究导向的硕士课程，献给希望以严谨方法，探究治理与社会如何被数字系统重塑的人。

Award学位
MRes研究型硕士 MRes
Level & credits层级与学分

Duration学制

Faculty所属学院
Digital Governance, Law & Global Affairs数字治理、法律与全球事务
Why this programme课程定位

Research digital society.研究数字社会。

Strong research is the foundation of good governance in the digital age — yet the questions are new and the methods are still forming.在数字时代，扎实的研究是良好治理的根基——而这些问题是新的，方法也仍在成形之中。

This programme trains you to design and carry out original research at the intersection of governance, data and society.本课程训练你在治理、数据与社会的交汇处，设计并完成原创研究。

Who it's for适合人群

For future researchers.为未来的研究者而设。

Aspiring researchers and analysts, and those preparing for a PhD or research-intensive careers in policy, government or academia.有志于研究与分析的人，以及准备攻读博士、或从事政策、政府与学术等研究密集型职业的人。

Learning outcomes学习成果

What you'll be able to do.毕业时你将能够……

Research design研究设计
  • Design rigorous, ethical research on digital governance and society针对数字治理与社会，设计严谨、合乎伦理的研究
  • Select and apply appropriate qualitative and quantitative methods选择并运用恰当的定性与定量方法
Analytical skills分析能力
  • Critically evaluate evidence, theory and data批判性评估证据、理论与数据
  • Produce an original, substantial piece of research完成一项原创而有分量的研究
Professional专业能力
  • Communicate research to academic and policy audiences向学术与政策受众传达研究成果
  • Work independently to research-degree standards以研究型学位的标准独立工作
Programme structure项目结构

A research-intensive year.以研究为核心的一年。

Advanced methods training, a focused taught element, and a substantial supervised thesis.高级方法训练、精选的授课模块，以及一篇分量十足的导师指导论文。

Research training研究训练
Core · methods & design核心 · 方法与设计
Research Design & Methods · Data & Quantitative Analysis · Research Ethics & Integrity研究设计与方法 · 数据与定量分析 · 研究伦理与诚信
Field element领域模块
Selected modules精选模块
Two modules chosen from the faculty's taught Master's, aligned to your research topic.从本院授课型硕士中选修两门，与你的研究主题对接。
Thesis学位论文
Supervised research · major component导师指导研究 · 主体部分
An original supervised research thesis — the principal element of the programme.一篇原创的、导师指导的研究论文——本课程的主体。

Designed as a direct pathway to doctoral study (DGov or PhD).本课程亦为博士阶段（DGov 或 PhD）的直接通道。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Research-led supervision, methods workshops and a seminar-based research community. Assessment is by research outputs — a methods portfolio, a research proposal and the final thesis.以研究为导向的导师指导、方法工作坊，以及研讨式的研究社群。评估以研究成果为主——方法作品集、研究计划书与最终论文。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in a relevant discipline; a clear research interest is expected. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.相关学科的良好荣誉学位(UK 2:1 或同等)；需具备明确的研究兴趣。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Doctoral study, and research and analyst roles in government, think tanks, international organisations and academia.攻读博士，或在政府、智库、国际组织与学术界从事研究与分析。

A direct route to the faculty's PhD or Professional Doctorate (DGov).直通本院哲学博士（PhD）或专业博士（DGov）。
