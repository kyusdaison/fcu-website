Digital Economy数字经济 (MSc)

Understand and shape the economy now emerging — how digital technologies, platforms and data are remaking markets, firms, work and value.理解并塑造正在涌现的经济形态——数字技术、平台与数据如何重塑市场、企业、工作与价值。

Why this programme课程定位

Read the economy that's emerging.读懂正在涌现的经济。

Digital technologies are not just new tools — they are changing the structure of markets, the nature of firms and the meaning of work and value.数字技术不只是新工具——它正在改变市场的结构、企业的本质，以及工作与价值的意义。

This programme gives you the economic theory, data skills and judgement to analyse the digital economy and to lead within it.本课程赋予你分析数字经济、并在其中担当领导所需的经济学理论、数据能力与判断力。

Who it's for适合人群

For analysts and leaders of the digital economy.为数字经济的分析者与领导者而设。

Graduates in economics, business, the social sciences or quantitative disciplines — and professionals working in policy, strategy, technology or finance.经济、商科、社会科学或定量学科的毕业生，以及在政策、战略、科技或金融领域工作的专业人士。

Curriculum课程结构

180 credits across three terms.三学期 · 180 学分。

Economic foundations, applied analysis and a substantial project — grounded in real markets.经济学基础、应用分析与一个分量十足的项目——立足于真实市场。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Lectures, applied seminars, data workshops and practitioner-led sessions. Assessment is by coursework — analytical assignments, a policy or strategy brief, a data project and the final capstone.讲授、应用研讨、数据工作坊与实务嘉宾讲授。评估以课程作业为主——分析作业、政策或战略简报、数据项目与毕业项目。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in economics, business, the social sciences or a quantitative discipline. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.经济、商科、社会科学或定量学科的良好荣誉学位(UK 2:1 或同等)。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Economic and policy analysis, strategy, consulting, and roles across technology, finance, government and international organisations.经济与政策分析、战略、咨询，以及科技、金融、政府与国际组织等领域的岗位。

Progression to the faculty's Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) or PhD.可衔接本院工商管理博士（DBA）或哲学博士（PhD）。
