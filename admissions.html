Admissions招生

How to join Future Citizen University.如何加入未来公民大学。

One clear route — from choosing a programme to enrolment — for undergraduate, Master's and doctoral applicants.一条清晰统一的路径——从选择课程到完成注册——面向本科、硕士与博士申请者。

How to apply如何申请

Five steps to your place.录取的五个步骤。

The same process applies across the University, with requirements tailored to each programme and level.全校采用同一套流程，具体要求依各课程与层级而定。

01

Choose your programme选择课程

Browse the faculties and programmes and identify the degree that fits your goals.浏览各学院与课程，确定契合你目标的学位。

02

Check entry requirements核对入学要求

Each programme page sets out its academic and English-language requirements.每个课程页面都说明其学术与英语要求。

03

Prepare your documents准备申请材料

Transcripts, degree certificates, a personal statement — and, for research routes, a proposal.成绩单、学位证书、个人陈述；研究型路径还需研究计划。

04

Submit your application提交申请

Send your application and supporting documents to the admissions office.将申请与证明材料提交至招生办公室。

05

Decision & enrolment录取与注册

Receive your decision, accept your offer and complete enrolment for your intake.收到录取结果，接受录取，并按入学季完成注册。

Applications are considered on a rolling basis. Contact the admissions office for the next available intake and current deadlines.申请采取滚动审核。请联系招生办公室了解最近的入学季与当前截止日期。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

Undergraduate honours degrees — school-leaving qualifications such as A-levels, the International Baccalaureate, or an equivalent recognised qualification (including a numerate subject where relevant).本科荣誉学位——A-Level、IB（国际文凭）或同等受认可的中学毕业资历（必要时含一门数理科目）。

Master's degrees — a good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in a relevant or related field.硕士学位——相关或相近领域的良好荣誉学位（UK 2:1 或同等标准）。

Doctoral degrees — a relevant Master's degree and a strong, original research proposal.博士学位——相关的硕士学位，以及扎实、原创的研究计划。

Specific requirements are listed on each具体要求请见每个 programme page课程页面.

English language英语要求

Studying in English.以英语学习。

Programmes are taught and assessed in English. Applicants whose first language is not English are asked to demonstrate proficiency — typically IELTS 6.0 for undergraduate study and IELTS 6.5 for postgraduate study, or an accepted equivalent. Some programmes may set a higher requirement.课程以英语授课与评估。母语非英语的申请者需证明英语水平——本科通常要求 IELTS 6.0，研究生通常要求 IELTS 6.5，或受认可的同等成绩。部分课程可能要求更高。

International applicants国际申请者

Applying from anywhere.来自世界各地的申请。

Future Citizen University warmly welcomes international applicants and accepts a wide range of international qualifications, assessed for equivalence. Guidance on documents, recognition of prior study and any study-visa requirements is available from the admissions office on request.未来公民大学诚挚欢迎国际申请者，并接受广泛的国际学历，按同等标准评估。有关材料、既往学历认定以及任何学习签证要求的指引，可向招生办公室索取。

Admissions support招生支持

Guidance for clear academic decisions.支持清晰的学术选择。

Admissions guidance is designed to help applicants understand the University's academic routes, prepare appropriate evidence and make informed decisions about programme level, faculty home and research direction.招生指导旨在帮助申请者理解大学的学术路径，准备适当材料，并就课程层级、学院归属与研究方向作出清晰选择。

01

Route advising路径建议

Applicants can ask which programme level and faculty route best fits their current preparation and aims.申请者可以咨询哪一课程层级与学院路径最符合其当前准备与目标。

02

Document guidance材料指引

The admissions office can clarify transcripts, certificates, statements and supporting evidence before submission.招生办公室可在提交前说明成绩单、证书、陈述与辅助材料要求。

03

Research preparation研究准备

Research applicants are encouraged to refine the question, evidence base and supervisory fit of their proposal.研究型申请者应进一步明确研究问题、证据基础与导师方向的契合度。

04

Enrolment readiness注册准备

Offer holders receive guidance on acceptance, fee confirmation, enrolment steps and preparation for the intake.获得录取的申请者将收到接受录取、学费确认、注册步骤与入学准备指引。

Learning support学习支持

Support continues through study, research and progression.支持贯穿学习、研究与进阶。

After admission, students are supported through academic advising, learning resources, supervision routines and progression planning. The aim is to make expectations clear and help each student connect their programme with a serious intellectual direction.入学之后，学生通过学术指导、学习资源、导师机制与进阶规划获得支持。其目标是让学习期待保持清晰，并帮助每位学生把课程同严肃的思想方向连接起来。

01

Academic advising学术指导

Advising helps students interpret programme requirements, choose suitable routes and connect modules with longer-term academic purpose.学术指导帮助学生理解课程要求、选择合适路径，并把模块学习同更长期的学术目标连接起来。

02

Learning resources学习资源

Students are guided toward library resources, digital research materials, study skills and the academic practices expected at their level.学生将获得图书馆资源、数字研究材料、学习技能以及相应层级学术实践方面的指引。

03

Research supervision研究指导

MRes and doctoral students work through supervision, milestones, methods development and scholarly review.MRes 与博士学生通过导师指导、里程碑、方法能力发展与学术审阅推进研究。

04

Progression planning进阶规划

Students are encouraged to plan clearly for professional entry, Master's study, research formation or doctoral contribution.大学鼓励学生清晰规划进入专业领域、攻读硕士、形成研究能力或作出博士层面贡献的路径。

Planning your investment.规划你的投入。
