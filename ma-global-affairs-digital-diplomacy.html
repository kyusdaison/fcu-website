Why this programme课程定位

Statecraft in a networked world.网络世界中的治国之道。

Technology has become both an instrument and an arena of global affairs — from digital diplomacy and data flows to cyber security and the governance of the internet.科技已成为全球事务的工具与舞台——从数字外交、数据流动，到网络安全与互联网治理。

Graduates learn to operate where international relations, technology and communication meet.毕业生学会在国际关系、科技与传播的交汇处行事。

Who it's for适合人群

For those who act on the global stage.为在全球舞台行事的人而设。

Diplomats, foreign-affairs and international-organisation professionals, journalists and analysts, and graduates in international relations, politics, law or communication.外交官、外事与国际组织从业者、记者与分析师，以及国际关系、政治、法律或传播背景的毕业生。

Learning outcomes学习成果

What you'll be able to do.毕业时你将能够……

Knowledge & understanding知识与理解
  • Analyse how digital technology reshapes diplomacy, security and the international order分析数字技术如何重塑外交、安全与国际秩序
  • Understand the global governance of data, networks and emerging technologies理解数据、网络与新兴技术的全球治理
Analytical skills分析能力
  • Assess geopolitical and technological risk评估地缘政治与技术风险
  • Evaluate strategy across statecraft, security and digital domains在治国、安全与数字领域评估战略
Practical & professional实践与专业
  • Produce briefings, strategy and diplomatic communication产出简报、战略与外交沟通文本
  • Represent and negotiate across cultural and institutional boundaries跨文化、跨机构地代表与谈判

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Diplomatic simulations, negotiation exercises, the case method and practitioner-led sessions. Assessment uses policy and strategy briefs, analyses, simulations and the final capstone.外交模拟、谈判演练、案例教学与实务嘉宾讲授。评估以政策与战略简报、分析、模拟与毕业项目为主。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in any relevant discipline; relevant professional experience is considered. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.任一相关学科的良好荣誉学位(UK 2:1 或同等)；相关工作经验可酌情认可。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Diplomatic service, foreign ministries, international organisations, NGOs, global public affairs, risk analysis, journalism and consultancy.外交部门、外交部、国际组织、非政府组织、全球公共事务、风险分析、新闻与咨询。

Progression to the faculty's Professional Doctorate (DGov) or PhD.可衔接本院专业博士（DGov）或哲学博士（PhD）。
