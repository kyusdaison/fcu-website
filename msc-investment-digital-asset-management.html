Investment & Digital Asset Management投资与数字资产管理 (MSc)

The theory and practice of investing across traditional and digital assets — portfolios, markets, risk and the rise of tokenised finance.跨越传统与数字资产的投资理论与实务——投资组合、市场、风险，以及通证化金融的崛起。

Why this programme课程定位

Invest across the old and new economy.在新旧经济之间布局投资。

Investing now spans both traditional markets and a fast-growing universe of digital and tokenised assets.如今的投资，既覆盖成熟市场，也涵盖快速增长的数字与通证化资产宇宙。

This programme builds rigorous investment and portfolio skills, and extends them to digital assets, so you can analyse, manage and govern capital across both worlds.本课程打造严谨的投资与组合能力，并将其延伸至数字资产，让你能够在两个世界中分析、管理并治理资本。

Who it's for适合人群

For investment professionals of the digital age.为数字时代的投资专业人士而设。

Graduates in finance, economics or quantitative disciplines — and professionals in investment, asset management and analysis.金融、经济或定量学科的毕业生，以及在投资、资产管理与分析领域的专业人士。

Curriculum课程结构

180 credits across three terms.三学期 · 180 学分。

Investment foundations, digital assets and a substantial project — across both markets.投资基础、数字资产与一个分量十足的项目——横跨两个市场。

Core modules · 60 credits核心模块 · 60 学分

Term 2第二学期
Core · 30 credits核心 · 30 学分

Capstone · 60 credits毕业项目 · 60 学分

A shared Future Citizen Core seminar runs through the year, alongside the modules above.贯穿全年的“未来公民核心”研讨，与上述模块并行。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Lectures, analytical labs, case studies and practitioner-led sessions. Assessment is by coursework — portfolio and analysis assignments, an investment report, and the final capstone.讲授、分析实验、案例研究与实务嘉宾讲授。评估以课程作业为主——组合与分析作业、投资报告，以及毕业项目。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in finance, economics or a quantitative discipline. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.金融、经济或定量学科的良好荣誉学位(UK 2:1 或同等)。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Asset and investment management, digital-asset funds, analysis and research, and risk roles across finance.资产与投资管理、数字资产基金、分析与研究，以及金融领域的风险岗位。

Progression to the faculty's Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) or PhD.可衔接本院工商管理博士（DBA）或哲学博士（PhD）。
