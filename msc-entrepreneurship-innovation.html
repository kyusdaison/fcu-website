Entrepreneurship & Innovation创业与创新 (MSc)

Turn ideas into ventures — the strategy, finance, technology and leadership of building new businesses and innovating inside organisations.把想法变成企业——构建新事业、并在组织内部创新所需的战略、金融、技术与领导力。

Why this programme课程定位

Build ventures that matter.创建真正重要的事业。

New value is increasingly created by ventures — startups, spin-outs and the innovators inside organisations.新的价值越来越多地由“创业”创造——初创、孵化项目，以及既有组织内部的创新者。

This programme teaches the craft of entrepreneurship end to end: spotting opportunity, building the product, raising finance, and scaling responsibly.本课程完整地教授创业之道:发现机会、打造产品、募集资金，并负责任地扩张。

Who it's for适合人群

For entrepreneurs and innovators.为创业者与创新者而设。

Aspiring entrepreneurs from any discipline, and professionals leading innovation, products or new ventures inside organisations.来自任何学科、有志创业的人，以及在组织中主导创新、产品或新事业的专业人士。

  • Innovate responsibly and sustainably负责任、可持续地进行创新

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Workshops, live venture projects, mentoring and practitioner-led sessions. Assessment is largely by coursework — a business model and plan, an investor pitch, and the final venture capstone or dissertation.工作坊、真实创业项目、导师辅导与实务嘉宾讲授。评估以课程作业为主——商业模式与计划、投资人路演，以及最终的创业毕业项目或学位论文。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in any discipline, with strong motivation to build or lead ventures. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.任何学科的良好荣誉学位(UK 2:1 或同等)，并具备创建或带领事业的强烈动机。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Launching and scaling ventures, venture capital and incubation, product and innovation leadership, and intrapreneurship inside larger organisations.创建与扩张事业、风险投资与孵化、产品与创新领导，以及在大型组织内部创业。

Progression to the faculty's Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) or PhD.可衔接本院工商管理博士（DBA）或哲学博士（PhD）。
