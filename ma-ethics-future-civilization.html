Ethics & Future Civilization伦理与未来文明 (MA)

Think rigorously about the ethical and civilizational questions of a technological age — values, society and the long-term future.就技术时代的伦理与文明问题进行严谨思考——价值、社会与长远的未来。

Why this programme课程定位

Think about how we should live.思考我们应当如何生活。

Technology is racing ahead of our shared thinking about values, society and the long-term future of civilization.技术正快速超越我们对价值、社会与文明长远未来的共同思考。

This programme trains you to reason carefully about ethics and civilization, and to bring that judgement to the institutions shaping our future.本课程训练你就伦理与文明进行审慎推理，并将这份判断力带入那些塑造未来的机构。

Who it's for适合人群

For ethical thinkers and future-shapers.为伦理的思考者与未来的塑造者而设。

Graduates from any discipline who want to think deeply about ethics, society and the future — in policy, technology, governance or public life.来自任何学科、希望就伦理、社会与未来深入思考的毕业生——无论身处政策、技术、治理还是公共生活。

180 credits across three terms.三学期 · 180 学分。

Foundations, applied ethics and a substantial project — careful thinking about hard questions.基础、应用伦理与一个分量十足的项目——就艰难问题进行审慎思考。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Seminars, close reading, structured debate and writing workshops. Assessment is by coursework — essays, a policy or position paper, and the final dissertation or applied project.研讨、精读、结构化辩论与写作工作坊。评估以课程作业为主——论文、政策或立场文件，以及最终的学位论文或应用项目。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in any discipline, with strong reading and writing ability. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5 (or higher).任何学科的良好荣誉学位(UK 2:1 或同等)，具备扎实的阅读与写作能力。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5(或更高)。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Ethics and policy advisory, governance and responsible innovation, research, education, and leadership in public and civic life.伦理与政策咨询、治理与负责任创新、研究、教育，以及公共与公民生活中的领导岗位。

Progression to the faculty's Professional Doctorate (DProf) or PhD.可衔接本院专业博士（DProf）或哲学博士（PhD）。
