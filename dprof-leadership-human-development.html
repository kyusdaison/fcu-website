Leadership & Human Development领导力与人类发展 (DProf)

A doctorate for senior leaders and practitioners — producing original, doctoral-level research on real problems of leadership and human development.为资深领导者与实践者而设的博士学位——就领导力与人类发展的真实问题，产出博士层级的原创研究。

Why this programme课程定位

Research from the front line of leadership.从领导实践前线出发的研究。

The deepest questions about leading and developing people are met by those who do it — who rarely have the framework to research them at depth.关于如何领导与培育他人，最深刻的问题，往往落在身体力行者身上——他们却少有框架去深入研究。

This doctorate gives senior practitioners the methods and supervision to turn a problem of practice into original, rigorous and defensible research.本博士为资深从业者提供方法与导师指导，把实践问题转化为原创、严谨而经得起检验的研究。

Who it's for适合人群

For senior leaders and practitioners.为资深领导者与从业者而设。

Senior leaders, managers, coaches, educators and people-development professionals who want to research a problem of practice at doctoral level.希望在博士层级研究实践问题的资深领导者、管理者、教练、教育者与人才发展专业人士。

Learning outcomes学习成果

What you'll be able to do.毕业时你将能够……

Knowledge & understanding知识与理解
  • Command the theory and methods underpinning leadership and development掌握支撑领导力与发展的理论与方法
  • Situate a problem of practice within the research literature将实践问题置于研究文献中定位
Research研究能力
  • Design and conduct rigorous, ethical research on practice就实践设计并开展严谨、合乎伦理的研究
  • Make an original contribution to knowledge and practice对知识与实践作出原创贡献
Leadership领导力
  • Translate research into organisational and human change将研究转化为组织与人的变革
  • Lead responsibly, reflectively and with evidence以反思与证据为本、负责任地领导
Programme structure项目结构

Doctoral study built around your practice.围绕你的实践而构建的博士研究。

A structured taught and research-training phase, followed by an original practice research thesis.先是结构化的授课与研究训练阶段，继而完成一篇原创的实践研究论文。

Taught phase授课阶段
Structured · methods & advanced study结构化 · 方法与高阶学习
Advanced Leadership & Development Theory · Research Design for Practice · Doctoral Methods & Ethics高级领导力与发展理论 · 面向实践的研究设计 · 博士方法与伦理
Research phase研究阶段
Supervised practice research导师指导的实践研究
A sustained, supervised research project on a problem of leadership or human development in practice.一项持续的、导师指导的、针对领导力或人类发展实践问题的研究项目。
Thesis & viva论文与答辩
Doctoral thesis博士论文
An original thesis defended by viva voce.一篇通过口试（viva）答辩的原创论文。

Designed to be undertaken alongside a senior leadership or practice role.可与资深领导或实务岗位并行修读。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Cohort-based residencies and seminars, individual supervision, and a community of senior practitioners. Assessment is by research milestones, the thesis and viva voce.以同期同学为核心的集中授课与研讨、个人导师指导，以及资深从业者社群。评估以研究里程碑、论文与口试答辩为主。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A Master's degree (or equivalent) and substantial senior professional experience; a research area aligned to practice. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.硕士学位(或同等)且具备丰富的资深专业经验；研究方向需与实践相关。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Advancement into the most senior leadership, people and development roles, with research that strengthens practice.迈向最高层级的领导、人才与发展岗位，并以研究强化实践。

A terminal doctoral qualification for senior practice.面向资深实践的终极博士资历。
