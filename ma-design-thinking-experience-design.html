Why this programme课程定位

Design for people.为人而设计。

Good products, services and experiences are not accidents — they are designed, with rigour, empathy and iteration.优秀的产品、服务与体验并非偶然——它们是以严谨、同理与迭代设计出来的。

This programme teaches human-centred design end to end: understanding people, framing problems, prototyping solutions and designing experiences that work.本课程完整地教授以人为本的设计:理解人、界定问题、制作原型，并设计真正可行的体验。

Who it's for适合人群

For designers and experience makers.为设计师与体验创造者而设。

Aspiring and practising designers from any background who want a rigorous foundation in design thinking and experience design.来自任何背景、有志或在职、希望在设计思维与体验设计上打下严谨基础的设计师。

Curriculum课程结构

180 credits across three terms.三学期 · 180 学分。

Foundations, service and impact, and a substantial design project — learning by doing.基础、服务与成效，以及一个分量十足的设计项目——在实做中学习。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Studio-based learning, design critiques, live projects and practitioner-led sessions. Assessment is by coursework — design projects, a portfolio, and the final capstone.以工作室为核心的学习、设计评图、真实项目与实务嘉宾讲授。评估以课程作业为主——设计项目、作品集，以及毕业项目。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in any discipline; a portfolio is welcomed. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.任何学科的良好荣誉学位(UK 2:1 或同等)；欢迎提供作品集。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

UX, product and service design, design strategy and research, and innovation and design leadership.用户体验、产品与服务设计、设计战略与研究，以及创新与设计领导。

Progression to the faculty's Professional Doctorate in Design & Media (DProf) or PhD.可衔接本院设计与媒体专业博士（DProf）或哲学博士（PhD）。
