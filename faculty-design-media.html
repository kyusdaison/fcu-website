Faculty research themes学院研究主题

Research gives this faculty its intellectual centre.研究为本学院提供思想中心。

These themes organise faculty seminars, MRes formation, doctoral supervision and public-purpose inquiry in the field.这些主题组织本领域的学院研讨、MRes 研究形成、博士导师指导与面向公共目标的探究。

01

Strategic communication战略传播

How institutions communicate with clarity, credibility and public consequence.研究机构如何以清晰、可信且具有公共影响的方式沟通。

02

Digital media systems数字媒体系统

Content systems, audience behaviour and the platform conditions of contemporary media.研究内容系统、受众行为与当代媒体的平台条件。

03

Design and experience设计与体验

Human-centred design, service experience and the practical testing of better systems.研究以人为中心的设计、服务体验与更优系统的实践检验。

04

Institutional narrative and identity机构叙事与身份

Brand, narrative, visual identity and the public meaning of institutions over time.研究品牌、叙事、视觉身份与机构长期公共意义。
