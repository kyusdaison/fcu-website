Computing & Intelligent Systems计算与智能系统 (MRes)

A research-intensive Master's for those who want to investigate the frontiers of computing, AI and intelligent systems.一门研究导向的硕士课程,献给希望探究计算、AI 与智能系统前沿的人。

Why this programme课程定位

Research the computing frontier.研究计算的前沿。

The pace of progress in computing and AI is set by research — rigorous, original and methodical.计算与 AI 的进步速度,由研究决定——严谨、原创而有方法。

This programme trains you to design and carry out original research in computing and intelligent systems, as a foundation for a PhD or a research career.本课程训练你在计算与智能系统领域设计并完成原创研究,作为攻读博士或研究生涯的基础。

Who it's for适合人群

For future researchers.为未来的研究者而设。

Strong computing graduates preparing for doctoral study or research-intensive roles in industry or academia.计算机基础扎实、准备攻读博士或从事业界/学术研究密集型工作的优秀毕业生。

Learning outcomes学习成果

What you'll be able to do.毕业时你将能够……

Research design研究设计
  • Design rigorous, ethical research in computing and AI在计算与 AI 领域设计严谨、合乎伦理的研究
  • Select and apply appropriate experimental and analytical methods选择并运用恰当的实验与分析方法
Technical skills技术能力
  • Implement and evaluate research-grade systems and experiments实现并评估达研究水准的系统与实验
  • Produce an original, substantial piece of research完成一项原创而有分量的研究
Professional专业能力
  • Communicate research to technical and scholarly audiences向技术与学术受众传达研究成果
  • Work independently to research-degree standards以研究型学位的标准独立工作
Programme structure项目结构

A research-intensive year.以研究为核心的一年。

Advanced methods, a focused taught element, and a substantial supervised thesis.高级方法、精选授课模块,以及一篇分量十足的导师指导论文。

Research training研究训练
Core · methods & systems核心 · 方法与系统
Research Methods · Advanced Topics in Computing & AI · Research Ethics & Integrity研究方法 · 计算与 AI 高级专题 · 研究伦理与诚信
Field element领域模块
Selected modules精选模块
Modules chosen from the faculty's taught Master's, aligned to your research topic.从本院授课型硕士中选修若干,与你的研究主题对接。
Thesis学位论文
Supervised research · major component导师指导研究 · 主体部分
An original supervised research thesis — the principal element of the programme.一篇原创的、导师指导的研究论文——本课程的主体。

Designed as a direct pathway to doctoral study (DProf or PhD).本课程亦为博士阶段（DProf 或 PhD）的直接通道。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Research-led supervision, methods and systems workshops, and a seminar-based research community. Assessment is by research outputs — a methods portfolio, a research proposal and the final thesis.以研究为导向的导师指导、方法与系统工作坊,以及研讨式的研究社群。评估以研究成果为主——方法作品集、研究计划书与最终论文。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in computing or a related discipline; a clear research interest and programming ability. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.计算机或相关学科的良好荣誉学位(英式 2:1 或同等);具备明确的研究兴趣与编程能力。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Doctoral study, and research and R&D roles in industry and academia.攻读博士,以及业界与学术界的研究与研发岗位。

A direct route to the faculty's PhD or Professional Doctorate (DProf).直通本院哲学博士（PhD）或专业博士（DProf）。
