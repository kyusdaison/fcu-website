Honours Degree · Levels 4–6荣誉学位 · 第 4–6 级

Blockchain, Fintech & Sovereign Digital Infrastructure区块链、金融科技与主权数字基础设施 (BSc Hons)

An interfaculty honours degree for those who will build the financial and digital infrastructure of the future — combining blockchain, fintech, computing and economics across the University's faculties.一门跨学院的荣誉学位，为那些将构建未来金融与数字基础设施的人而设——将区块链、金融科技、计算与经济学跨越大学各学院融为一体。

Why this degree学位定位

Build sovereign digital infrastructure.构建主权数字基础设施。

The rails of money and trust are being rebuilt on new technology — blockchain, digital assets and the infrastructure that nations and institutions increasingly depend on.货币与信任的轨道，正被新技术重建——区块链、数字资产，以及国家与机构日益依赖的基础设施。

This honours degree is interfaculty by design: you learn to build the technology, understand the economics and grasp the governance of sovereign digital systems.本荣誉学位以跨学院为设计原则:你将学会构建技术、理解经济，并把握主权数字系统的治理。

Who it's for适合人群

For the builders of digital infrastructure.为数字基础设施的建设者而设。

Ambitious, numerate school-leavers and transfer students who want to build at the intersection of technology, finance and sovereign systems.志向远大、具备数理基础的中学毕业生与转学学生，渴望在技术、金融与主权系统的交汇处构建。

Programme structure项目结构

360 credits across three years.三年 · 360 学分。

An interfaculty foundation, a specialist core, and an honours year — Levels 4 to 6.跨学院的基础、专业核心，以及荣誉之年——第 4 级至第 6 级。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Lectures, labs, build projects and group work, with an interfaculty capstone in the final year. Assessment blends coursework, build projects and examinations.讲授、实验、构建项目与小组协作，并在最后一年完成一个跨学院的毕业项目。评估融合课程作业、构建项目与考试。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

School-leaving qualifications such as A-levels, the International Baccalaureate, or an equivalent recognised qualification, including a numerate subject. English equivalent to IELTS 6.0. International applicants are warmly welcomed.A-Level、IB（国际文凭）或同等受认可的中学毕业资历，需含一门数理科目。英语相当于 IELTS 6.0。诚挚欢迎国际申请者。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Blockchain and fintech engineering, digital-infrastructure and sovereign-systems roles, financial technology, startups and the public sector — and a strong foundation for postgraduate study.区块链与金融科技工程、数字基础设施与主权系统岗位、金融科技、初创与公共部门——并为研究生阶段打下坚实基础。

Progression to the University's Master's programmes (MSc, MA and MRes) across all five faculties.可衔接本校五大学院的硕士课程（MSc、MA 与 MRes）。
