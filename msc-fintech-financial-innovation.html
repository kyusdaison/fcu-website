Fintech & Financial Innovation金融科技与金融创新 (MSc)

Where finance meets technology — design, build and lead the next generation of payments, digital assets, open banking and financial services.金融与技术交汇之处——设计、构建并引领下一代支付、数字资产、开放银行与金融服务。

Why this programme课程定位

Build the future of finance.构建金融的未来。

Finance is being rebuilt on new technology — payments, digital assets, open data and AI are reshaping who provides financial services and how.金融正被新技术重建——支付、数字资产、开放数据与 AI 正在重塑由谁、以何种方式提供金融服务。

This programme combines finance, technology and regulation so you can build financial innovation that is sound, compliant and genuinely useful.本课程将金融、技术与监管结合，让你能够构建稳健、合规而真正有用的金融创新。

Who it's for适合人群

For builders and leaders of financial innovation.为金融创新的构建者与领导者而设。

Graduates in finance, economics, computing or quantitative disciplines — and professionals moving into fintech, banking and digital-finance roles.金融、经济、计算机或定量学科的毕业生，以及转向金融科技、银行与数字金融岗位的专业人士。

Curriculum课程结构

180 credits across three terms.三学期 · 180 学分。

Finance and technology foundations, applied innovation and a substantial project.金融与技术基础、应用创新，以及一个分量十足的项目。

Term 1第一学期
Core modules · 60 credits核心模块 · 60 学分
Foundations of Finance · Financial Technology & Infrastructure · Data & Programming for Finance · Payments & Open Banking金融学基础 · 金融技术与基础设施 · 金融数据与编程 · 支付与开放银行
Term 2第二学期
Core · 30 credits核心 · 30 学分
Digital Assets & Tokenisation · Risk, Regulation & Compliance in Fintech数字资产与通证化 · 金融科技的风险、监管与合规
Options · choose 30 credits选修 · 任选 30 学分
Blockchain in Finance · AI in Financial Services · Quantitative & Algorithmic Trading · InsurTech & RegTech · Financial Inclusion金融区块链 · 金融服务中的 AI · 量化与算法交易 · 保险科技与监管科技 · 普惠金融
Term 3第三学期
Capstone · 60 credits毕业项目 · 60 学分
Dissertation, or an applied fintech project delivered with a partner organisation.学位论文，或与合作机构联合完成的应用型金融科技项目。

A shared Future Citizen Core seminar runs through the year, alongside the modules above.贯穿全年的“未来公民核心”研讨，与上述模块并行。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Lectures, hands-on labs, case studies and practitioner-led sessions. Assessment is largely by coursework — technical and analytical assignments, a product or strategy project, and the final capstone.讲授、动手实验、案例研究与实务嘉宾讲授。评估以课程作业为主——技术与分析作业、产品或战略项目，以及毕业项目。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in finance, economics, computing or a quantitative discipline. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5.金融、经济、计算机或定量学科的良好荣誉学位(UK 2:1 或同等)。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Fintech, banking, payments, digital assets, and innovation roles across financial services, technology and regulation.金融科技、银行、支付、数字资产，以及金融服务、科技与监管领域的创新岗位。

Progression to the faculty's Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) or PhD.可衔接本院工商管理博士（DBA）或哲学博士（PhD）。
