Honours Degree · Levels 4–6荣誉学位 · 第 4–6 级

Digital Governance, Identity & Global Affairs数字治理、身份与全球事务 (BA Hons)

An interfaculty honours degree for those who will govern, regulate and represent in the digital age — combining governance, digital identity, law and global affairs across the University's faculties.一门跨学院的荣誉学位，为那些将在数字时代治理、监管与代表的人而设——将治理、数字身份、法律与全球事务跨越大学各学院融为一体。

Why this degree学位定位

Govern the digital age.治理数字时代。

Power, identity and belonging are being remade by digital systems — and the institutions that govern them are still catching up.权力、身份与归属正被数字系统重塑——而治理它们的机构仍在追赶。

This honours degree is interfaculty by design: you learn how governance, law, identity and global affairs interlock, so you can shape public life in a digital world.本荣誉学位以跨学院为设计原则:你将理解治理、法律、身份与全球事务如何彼此咬合，从而在数字世界中塑造公共生活。

Who it's for适合人群

For the shapers of public life.为塑造公共生活的人而设。

Ambitious school-leavers and transfer students drawn to government, law, international affairs and the governance of technology — with an international outlook.志向远大的中学毕业生与转学学生，心向政府、法律、国际事务与科技治理，并具国际视野。

Programme structure项目结构

360 credits across three years.三年 · 360 学分。

An interfaculty foundation, a specialist core, and an honours year — Levels 4 to 6.跨学院的基础、专业核心，以及荣誉之年——第 4 级至第 6 级。

The Level 4 foundation is shared across the honours degrees, so you build a broad base before you specialise.第 4 级基础在各荣誉学位之间共享——你先建立宽广的根基，再走向专精。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Lectures, seminars, policy and diplomacy simulations, and group projects, with an interfaculty capstone in the final year. Assessment blends coursework, essays and examinations.讲授、研讨、政策与外交模拟，以及小组项目，并在最后一年完成一个跨学院的毕业项目。评估融合课程作业、论文与考试。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

School-leaving qualifications such as A-levels, the International Baccalaureate, or an equivalent recognised qualification. English equivalent to IELTS 6.0. International applicants are warmly welcomed.A-Level、IB（国际文凭）或同等受认可的中学毕业资历。英语相当于 IELTS 6.0。诚挚欢迎国际申请者。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Government, public policy, regulation, international organisations, diplomacy, and digital-governance and identity roles — and a strong foundation for postgraduate study.政府、公共政策、监管、国际组织、外交，以及数字治理与身份相关岗位——并为研究生阶段打下坚实基础。

Progression to the University's Master's programmes (MSc, MA and MRes) across all five faculties.可衔接本校五大学院的硕士课程（MSc、MA 与 MRes）。
