Educating a new kind of public-sector and policy professional — one who can design, evaluate and lead governance in the digital age, at the intersection of public policy, technology, law and institutions.培养新一代公共部门与政策专业人才——能在数字时代设计、评估并领导治理，立于公共政策、科技、法律与制度的交汇之处。

Why this programme课程定位

Govern the digital age.治理数字时代。

The questions that now define public life — digital identity, AI in public decision-making, platform regulation, data and sovereignty — fall between disciplines. This programme is built to govern them as a whole.如今界定公共生活的那些问题——数字身份、公共决策中的 AI、平台监管、数据与主权——都落在学科之间。本课程正是为把它们作为一个整体来治理而设。

It pairs the rigour of public-policy analysis with real fluency in technology, data, law and ethics, so graduates can both understand digital systems and shape the institutions that govern them.它将公共政策分析的严谨，与对科技、数据、法律与伦理的真正通晓结合起来:让毕业生既读得懂数字系统，也塑造得了治理这些系统的制度。

Who it's for适合人群

For the people who shape public life.为塑造公共生活的人而设。

Civil servants, policy and regulatory professionals, advisors, and recent graduates of any discipline aiming for leadership roles in digital-age government and institutions.在职公务员、政策与监管人员、顾问，以及任何学科、有志于在数字时代的政府与机构中担任领导角色的应届毕业生。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Seminars, the case method, policy simulations, project-based learning and practitioner-led sessions. Assessment is authentic and continuous — policy briefs, analytical assignments, group projects, presentations and the final capstone — with little reliance on closed-book examination.研讨课、案例教学、政策模拟、项目制学习与实务嘉宾讲授。评估以真实情境、过程性为主——政策简报、分析作业、小组项目、答辩与毕业项目——基本不依赖闭卷考试。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A good honours degree (UK 2:1 or equivalent) in any discipline — the programme is interdisciplinary by design and welcomes backgrounds in law, social sciences, computing, economics and the humanities. Relevant professional experience is considered. English proficiency equivalent to IELTS 6.5.任一学科的良好荣誉学位(UK 2:1 或同等)——本课程以跨学科为设计原则，欢迎法律、社会科学、计算机、经济学与人文背景。相关工作经验可酌情认可。英语水平相当于 IELTS 6.5。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Graduates move into policy and advisory roles, digital-government and GovTech teams, regulators, public affairs, international organisations, think tanks and consultancies.毕业生可进入政策与顾问岗位、数字政府与 GovTech 团队、监管机构、公共事务、国际组织、智库与咨询机构。

Progression to the faculty's Professional Doctorate in Digital Governance (DGov) or PhD.可衔接本院数字治理专业博士（DGov）或哲学博士（PhD）。
