Why upgrade now为何此刻升级

Renewing a university for a world being rewritten.为一个正在被重写的世界，重塑一所大学。

Every few generations, the world is rewritten. We are living through one of those moments.每隔几代人，世界会迎来一次彻底的重写。今天，我们正身处这样一个时刻。

Digital systems, artificial intelligence and a new digital economy are reshaping how we govern, work, trade and belong — faster than any single discipline can explain. Yet most universities were built for a world of separate subjects and slow change.数字系统、人工智能与全新的数字经济，正在重塑我们如何治理、如何工作、如何交易、如何归属——其速度，已超出任何单一学科所能独自解释。然而，多数大学仍建立在一个学科分立、变化缓慢的旧世界之上。

How the upgrade works升级如何发生

The University is being organised as a knowledge architecture.大学正在被组织为一套知识架构。

FCU's upgrade is not a cosmetic refresh. It clarifies the institution's academic grammar: faculties, programmes, research pathways, public-facing knowledge, and partnerships that can support long-term institutional development.FCU 的升级不是表层美化。它澄清大学的学术语法:学院、课程、研究路径、面向公共领域的知识，以及支持长期机构发展的合作关系。

Curriculum architecture课程架构

Levels, credits and awards are presented as one coherent ladder.层级、学分与学位以一套连贯阶梯呈现。

Research progression研究进阶

MRes, professional doctorates and PhD routes make research pathways legible.MRes、专业博士与 PhD 让研究路径清晰可读。

Institutional partnerships机构合作

Partnerships are framed around upgrade, public value and academic capacity.合作围绕升级、公共价值与学术能力建设展开。

Civilizational horizon文明视野

The academic model connects knowledge, technology, institutions and future society.学术模式连接知识、技术、制度与未来社会。

Institutional horizon机构视野

A university upgraded to produce public academic capacity.一所升级为公共学术能力平台的大学。

FCU's visual and academic language is organised around institutional continuity, digital-era systems, applied research and international partnership — the elements of a university built for long-term public relevance.FCU 的学术表达围绕机构连续性、数字时代系统、应用研究与国际合作而组织——这些要素共同构成一所面向长期公共价值的大学。

Institutional continuity机构连续性

The upgrade strengthens an operating university rather than presenting a speculative future project.升级强化的是一所正在运营的大学，而不是一个停留在设想中的未来项目。

Digital public systems数字公共系统

Governance, infrastructure, identity, finance and communication are treated as connected systems of public life.治理、基础设施、身份、金融与传播被视为公共生活中彼此相连的系统。

Applied research capacity应用研究能力

Research pathways support original knowledge, professional practice and institutional problem-solving.研究路径支持原创知识、专业实践与机构问题解决。

Global academic partnership全球学术合作

The University invites serious collaboration with public, academic and institutional partners.大学欢迎与公共、学术及机构伙伴开展严肃合作。

The intersection交汇之处

Where the future is actually decided.未来，真正被决定的地方。

Traditional institutions divide these domains. The emerging world experiences them as one. FCU's upgrade brings them into one connected academic model.传统机构习惯把这些领域分而治之，新兴世界却把它们作为一个整体来经历。FCU 的升级，正是把它们纳入同一套互联的学术模式。
