The academic model学术模式

One connected academic model.一套互联的学术模式。

The University's academic grammar is deliberate and legible: faculties provide disciplinary depth, interfaculty pathways create connection, and research routes give advanced inquiry a clear institutional home.大学的学术结构经过有意设计，且清晰可读：学院提供学科深度，跨学院路径建立连接，研究路径则为高阶探究提供明确的学术归属。

Curriculum architecture课程架构

Levels, credits and awards are presented as one coherent ladder.层级、学分与学位以一套连贯阶梯呈现。

Research progression研究进阶

MRes, professional doctorates and PhD routes make research pathways legible.MRes、专业博士与 PhD 让研究路径清晰可读。

Institutional collaboration机构协作

Academic and institutional work is framed around shared public value.学术与机构协作围绕共同的公共价值展开。

Future society未来社会

The academic model connects knowledge, technology, institutions and the public questions ahead.学术模式连接知识、技术、制度与未来的公共问题。

Academic life学术生活

A learning environment shaped by seminars, advising and research practice.由研讨、指导与研究实践塑造的学习环境。

University life at FCU is organised around serious academic contact: seminar discussion, faculty advising, independent work, research resources and progression guidance. The aim is to make students' intellectual direction visible and supported from entry to advanced study.FCU 的大学学习生活围绕严肃的学术接触展开：研讨讨论、教师指导、独立工作、研究资源与进阶规划。其目标是让学生的思想方向从入学到高阶学习都清晰可见，并获得支持。

Seminar culture研讨文化

Small academic settings support close reading, argument, critique and intellectual confidence.小型学术场景支持精读、论证、批判与思想表达的自信。

Faculty advising教师指导

Advising helps students connect programme choices with methods, research direction and progression.教师指导帮助学生把课程选择同方法、研究方向与进阶路径连接起来。

Research resources研究资源

Students are guided toward library materials, digital resources, evidence practices and academic writing.学生获得图书资料、数字资源、证据实践与学术写作方面的指引。

Progression guidance进阶指导

Support continues from undergraduate foundations into Master's study, research formation and doctoral routes.支持从本科基础延伸至硕士学习、研究能力形成与博士路径。

The academic ladder学位层级

A clear structure, from first year to doctorate.清晰的学位架构，从大一直抵博士。

The structure is clear by design: each level has a distinct academic purpose, while the same interdisciplinary spirit runs through the whole ladder.这套结构清晰而有意：每一层级都有不同的学术目的，同时同一种跨学科精神贯穿整套阶梯。
