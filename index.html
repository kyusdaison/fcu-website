The academic model学术模式

One connected academic model.一套互联的学术模式。

The University's academic grammar is coherent by design: five faculties, a full ladder of programmes, and research pathways that connect knowledge, technology, institutions and society.大学的学术结构清晰一贯:五个学院、一套完整的学位阶梯，以及连接知识、技术、制度与社会的研究路径。

Curriculum architecture课程架构

Levels, credits and awards are presented as one coherent ladder.层级、学分与学位以一套连贯阶梯呈现。

Research progression研究进阶

MRes, professional doctorates and PhD routes make research pathways legible.MRes、专业博士与 PhD 让研究路径清晰可读。

Institutional collaboration机构协作

Academic and institutional work is framed around shared public value.学术与机构协作围绕共同的公共价值展开。

Civilizational horizon文明视野

The academic model connects knowledge, technology, institutions and future society.学术模式连接知识、技术、制度与未来社会。

Domains of inquiry探究领域

Six domains, studied as one system.六个领域，作为一个系统来学习。

FCU organises teaching and research around domains that increasingly shape public life. Each faculty gives disciplinary depth; interfaculty pathways teach students to move between them.FCU 围绕日益塑造公共生活的关键领域组织教学与研究。每个学院提供学科深度；跨学院路径则训练学生在不同领域之间移动。

Research culture研究文化

Evidence, systems and public purpose.证据、系统与公共目的。

Across the University, research is treated as a shared academic practice: seminars, supervised inquiry, data work, institutional analysis and written argument. Students learn how knowledge is produced, tested and carried into public life.在整所大学中，研究被视为一种共同的学术实践：研讨、导师指导的探究、数据工作、机构分析与书面论证。学生学习知识如何被生产、检验，并进入公共生活。

Seminar culture研讨文化

Small academic communities build careful reading, argument and critique.小型学术共同体培养细读、论证与批判能力。

Data and archives数据与档案

Research moves between empirical evidence, institutional records and digital systems.研究在经验证据、机构记录与数字系统之间展开。

Supervision导师指导

Advanced study is supported through structured supervision and research milestones.高阶学习通过结构化导师指导与研究里程碑推进。

Public value公共价值

Research questions are connected to institutions, society and long-term public purpose.研究问题连接机构、社会与长期公共目的。
