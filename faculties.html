The five faculties五大学院

Faculties at Future Citizen University.未来公民大学的学院。

The faculties give FCU its academic structure: each provides disciplinary depth, leads postgraduate and doctoral routes, and contributes to undergraduate honours study across the University.五大学院构成 FCU 的学术结构：每个学院提供学科深度，主导研究生与博士路径，并共同支撑全校本科荣誉学位。

Academic architecture学术架构

Disciplinary depth, interfaculty connection.学科深度，跨学院连接。

FCU is organised so that students can enter a clear faculty home without losing the wider connections between governance, technology, economy, human development and design. The result is a university structure that is readable, rigorous and connected to the systems shaping public life.FCU 的组织方式，使学生能够进入清晰的学院归属，同时不失治理、科技、经济、人类发展与设计之间的广阔连接。由此形成一套清晰、严谨，并与塑造公共生活的系统相连的大学结构。

Faculty home学院归属

Each programme has a disciplinary base and a clear academic home.每个课程都有学科基础与清晰的学术归属。

Programme leadership课程主导

Faculties lead Master's, MRes and doctoral routes in their fields.各学院主导本领域的硕士、MRes 与博士路径。

Research formation研究能力形成

Advanced study develops through methods, supervision and original inquiry.高阶学习通过方法、导师指导与原创探究形成。

Interfaculty study跨学院学习

Undergraduate honours degrees draw deliberately from all five faculties.本科荣誉学位有意汲取五大学院的知识与方法。

How the faculties work学院如何协同

A faculty structure that supports teaching, research and public purpose.支撑教学、研究与公共目标的学院结构。

The five faculties are not separate silos. They provide depth where depth is needed, and connection where the questions of the digital age require more than one discipline.五大学院并非彼此隔离的单位。它们在需要深度之处提供深度，在数字时代的问题需要多学科回应之处建立连接。

01

Teaching教学

Seminars, studios, labs and supervised projects help students build disciplinary command and interdisciplinary judgement.研讨、工作室、实验室与导师指导项目，帮助学生形成学科掌握与跨学科判断力。

02

Research研究

Faculty-led themes connect methods, evidence and public questions across the University.学院主导的研究主题，把方法、证据与公共问题在全校范围内连接起来。

03

Supervision导师指导

MRes and doctoral routes are supported through academic supervision, milestones and scholarly review.MRes 与博士路径由学术导师指导、研究里程碑与学术审阅共同支撑。

04

Public purpose公共目标

Knowledge is directed toward institutions, society and responsible action in the digital age.知识指向数字时代的机构、社会与负责任行动。
