Why this programme课程定位

Work at the frontier.在前沿之处探索。

The questions about how people grow, how we lead, and how civilizations endure have no settled answers — they need original research.关于人如何成长、我们如何领导、文明如何延续的那些问题，尚无定论——它们需要原创研究。

FCU's PhD supports rigorous doctoral work across human development, leadership, ethics and civilization studies, supervised by faculty active in these fields.FCU 的博士项目支持跨越人类发展、领导力、伦理与文明研究的严谨博士研究，由活跃于这些领域的师资指导。

Who it's for适合人群

For the next generation of researchers.为下一代研究者而设。

Outstanding graduates with a relevant Master's and a clear, original research proposal, pursuing an academic or advanced research career.持相关硕士学位、具备清晰原创研究计划，有志于学术或高阶研究生涯的优秀毕业生。

  • Contribute to the research community, and supervise or teach over time贡献于研究社群，并逐步指导或教学

Supervised original research.导师指导下的原创研究。

Teaching & assessment教学与评估

Learn by doing.在实践中学习。

Individual and team supervision, a structured research-training programme, and an active seminar and research culture. Assessment is by progression milestones, the thesis and viva voce.个人与团队导师指导、结构化的研究训练计划，以及活跃的研讨与研究文化。评估以阶段里程碑、论文与口试答辩为主。

Entry requirements入学要求

Who can apply.申请条件。

A relevant Master's degree at merit/distinction (or equivalent) and a strong, original research proposal. English equivalent to IELTS 6.5 (or higher for some areas).相关硕士学位且成绩良好/优异(或同等)，并有扎实、原创的研究计划。英语相当于 IELTS 6.5(部分方向要求更高)。

Careers & progression就业与升学

Where it leads.毕业去向。

Academic and research careers, and senior advisory, policy and leadership roles across the public, civic and private sectors.学术与研究生涯，以及公共、公民与私营部门的高阶顾问、政策与领导岗位。

The faculty's terminal research degree.本院的终极研究型学位。
